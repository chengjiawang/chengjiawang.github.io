I am now a Scientist in Machine Learning for Medical Imaging at BHF Centre for Cardiovascular Science, University of Edinburgh, in Prof. David Newby's team. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Machine Learning Based Medical Image Analysis in 2016 co-funded by School of Clinical Science, University of Edinburgh and Toshiba Medical Visualization System - Europe (Now Canon Medical Research Europe LTD), supervised by Dr. Scott Semple, Prof. David Newby in University of Edinburgh and Dr. Keith Goatman in Toshiba. I received two MSc degrees in Computer Vision from the two-year European Erasmus Mundus Master in Vision and Robotics (hosted by Heriot-Watt University, UK; University of Girona, Spain; University of Burgundy, France) in 2011, supervised by Prof. Yvan Petillot. I received a BEng degree in Electronic Information Engineering and a BA degree in English in 2009. I've been involved in or lead the machine learning research for over 10 clinical trials.
